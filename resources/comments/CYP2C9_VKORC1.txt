- COMMENTS
  1. The CYP2C9 and VKORC1 genotypes are major genetic determinants that can explain
     30-50% of the inter-individual variability in warfarin dose requirement.

  2. In Koreans, the CYP2C9 *1 and *3 allele frequencies are known to be 0.934 and 0.060,
     respectively, and the VKORC1 1173C allele frequency in Koreans has been reported as
     0.097.
     (Ref. Br J Clin Pharmacol 2005;60:418-22, Pharmacogenomics 2007;8:329-37)

  3. The above results were prepared using the Gene-specific Information Tables from
     ClinPGx. (https://www.clinpgx.org/page/cyp2c9RefMaterials,
     https://www.clinpgx.org/page/vkorc1RefMaterials, downloaded 2025.10.20)
